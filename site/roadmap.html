Strategic Framework

Build trust, then build infrastructure.

Our framework sequences verification before commitment: establish the protocol, relationships, and regulatory understanding first; align strategically; then develop physical infrastructure and scale operations. Each phase is gated by what the previous one proves — not by a calendar — and physical-infrastructure milestones are mechanically dependent on consent being verifiably in place.

The result is a deliberately patient on-ramp. The protocol can mature, partnerships can deepen, and feasibility can be demonstrated long before any steel touches the ground.

Four phases.

  • Regulatory pathway mapping under the Space (Launches and Returns) Act 2018
  • Formal partnership discussions and ILUA / consent groundwork
  • Tokenomics and governance framework design
  • Mainnet and physical coordination pilots
  • Ground infrastructure development through consent-aligned processes
  • ISRU and propellant-production studies leveraging Pilbara minerals
  • Recovery-range and reentry trials over southern ocean corridors
  • Integration with sovereign safety and regulatory frameworks
  • High-cadence launch, recovery, and refurbishment operations
  • Scaled multiplanetary coordination primitives
  • Lunar and Mars nodes using delay-tolerant networking
  • Full decentralisation of genesis stewardship into community governance

Timeframes are indicative and conceptual. The protocol is a living project; phases advance on verified progress and consent, not fixed dates.
