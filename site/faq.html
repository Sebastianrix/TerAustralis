The essentials about TerAustralis Incognita and the Starbase Down Under vision.

TerAustralis Incognita is a decentralized, open, and permissionless protocol for coordinating physical and digital assets across Earth-to-multiplanetary operations. It is the coordination layer beneath "Starbase Down Under" — a vision for high-cadence launch, recovery, refurbishment, and propellant production in the Southern Hemisphere, developed through genuine partnership with Indigenous communities.
No. We are not a launch company or satellite operator. We build the coordination layer — the protocol, primitives, and incentives — that make Starbase-scale operations faster, more open, and more abundant. We are designed to complement launch providers, operators, and sovereign regulators, not replace them.
Australia combines world-class critical-minerals provinces, favourable northern launch geography, southern ocean recovery ranges, vast sparsely-populated test areas, political stability, and an established civil-space regulatory framework. The Pilbara sits at the heart of that minerals strength — the "red dust" that the whole thesis carries toward the stars.
A Starline is the protocol's core primitive: a verifiable, ordered coordination path connecting nodes, assets, and milestones across Earth → orbit → interplanetary space. Each Starline bundles a Route (ordered waypoints), the Knowledge attached to each waypoint (blueprints, signed telemetry, simulations), and the Law that governs passage (rules, attestations, and consent conditions). A Starline is open only when every waypoint's Law conditions are satisfied.
Consent is native to the protocol, not a bolt-on. Routing can never bypass it: a path stays blocked until the relevant Law and consent conditions are verifiably met. Land-use and physical-infrastructure rewards are mechanically blocked until custodian consent (ILUA / free, prior and informed consent) is attested. All cultural framing is treated as provisional and consent-pending until genuine partnership with custodians and Prescribed Body Corporates is established.
$TINC (provisional) is the protocol's work-enabling utility token, kept to three narrow roles: coordination gas, staking collateral, and governance weight. Supply pairs a hard cap with a milestone-gated Builder Treasury that releases as physical and technical milestones are verifiably traversed. Detailed tokenomics — supply figures, allocation, and governance — will be published in a separate whitepaper during the Exploration phase, subject to legal and regulatory review.
We are in the Discovery phase: protocol specification, architecture refinement, stakeholder mapping, and builder recruitment. The litepaper is a living document. The site, vision, and visualizations are early-stage and conceptual, intended to communicate direction and invite aligned contributors.
