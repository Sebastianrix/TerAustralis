## The idea

In this project's symbolic system, certain songs function as **maps** — routes
encoded as music. This is the **Starline** idea (`starlines-spec.md`) and the
songline idea joined: *a song is a path made of many verses.* A song-map is a Starline
you can hum.

Where the music **themes** in `unifying-vision.md` describe *values* (one-but-many,
the yes, the spirit of flight), song-maps describe *routes* — movement through places
toward a destination.

## How a song-map relates to a Starline

- **Route** — the ordered places/movements the song passes through.
- **Knowledge** — the imagery and feeling that tell you how to travel it.
- **Law** — the spirit in which it's read (with care, with consent, as homage).

A song-map is the *human, felt* version of the Starline primitive — the same shape,
carried in melody instead of mechanism.

## For the keeper to fill in

For each song-map, the route is yours to trace — no one else can read your map for
you:

- Where does it **start**?
- Where does it **lead**?
- What are the **waypoints** along the way?

Add them in the table above whenever they come to you.
