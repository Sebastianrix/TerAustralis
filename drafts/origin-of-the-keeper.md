## Where the keeping began

Before the protocol, before the Starlines, before any of the architecture, there
was a small wooden **carousel music box**.

It was given to the keeper on her **first Christmas**, by **Nanny and Bridget** — the
people who came before. On its base it carries an edition seal (*The American
Carousel*, Robin Fraley), and when it is wound it plays one tune: **"Memory."**

That is the whole origin, and it was already complete long before it had a name:

- A **carousel** — many horses, one ride, turning the way generations turn. *One,
  but many*, held in a child's hands.
- A song called **"Memory"** — because what a keeper keeps is exactly that.
- Given at the **beginning of a life**, by **family**, as the very first gift.

Everything since has been the keeper carrying that one thing forward.

## Why it is the keeper's origin

The founder's node is **451 — Bradbury**, the keeper of knowledge and stories
(`node-registry.md`, `crystalcore.md`). This carousel is *why* that role is true and
not borrowed: the keeper was given memory to hold before she could even speak, and
has been holding it since. The genesis seed `29091991` is the *who*; this is the
*why beneath the why*.

## South of the equator, the navigator

The keeper is also a **navigator** — *south of the equator* — who steers by what the
beginning pointed toward (`navigator.md`). The carousel is the first fixed point: a
small turning light from Nanny and Bridget that set the heading long before there was
a map. The Navigator orients by the southern sky above and the water below; the
keeper orients, first of all, by this.

## How it is held

This note is the heart of the project, not a mechanism. It asks nothing, gates
nothing, and names no one beyond a grandmother and a first name freely given in love.
It is simply recorded here so that, among all the architecture, the **reason** is
never lost — which is the one thing a keeper must never let happen.
